## Interview Preparation Strategy

### By Company Type

**FAANG (Google, Facebook, Amazon, Apple, Netflix)**
- Focus on medium to hard DP problems
- Master 2D DP and optimization
- Practice explaining your thought process
- Time yourself: 30-45 minutes per problem

**Startups (Uber, Stripe, Airbnb)**
- Focus on practical problems
- Emphasize code quality and edge cases
- Be ready to discuss real-world applications
- System design might be as important as coding

### Preparation Timeline

#### 4 Weeks Before Interview
- Complete all Easy + Medium problems for target company
- Review DP patterns specific to that company

#### 2 Weeks Before
- Complete Hard problems
- Do mock interviews
- Practice explaining solutions

#### 1 Week Before
- Review your solutions
- Practice common follow-ups
- Rest well, confidence matters!

## Company-Specific Tips

### Google
- **Focus**: Algorithmic complexity, optimization
- **Tip**: Always discuss time/space complexity
- **Common follow-up**: "Can you optimize this further?"

### Facebook/Meta
- **Focus**: Real-world applications, scalability
- **Tip**: Relate solutions to Facebook products
- **Common follow-up**: "How would this scale to billions of users?"

### Amazon
- **Focus**: Leadership principles, practical solutions
- **Tip**: Mention trade-offs explicitly
- **Common follow-up**: "What if constraints change?"

### Microsoft
- **Focus**: Microsoft products, systematic approach
- **Tip**: Write clean, production-ready code
- **Common follow-up**: "How would you test this?"

### Apple
- **Focus**: Design, user experience impact
- **Tip**: Consider performance on devices
- **Common follow-up**: "How does this affect battery/memory?"
