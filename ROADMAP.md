## ðŸ’¡ Study Tips

1. **Consistency > Intensity**: 2 hours daily is better than 14 hours on Sunday
2. **Active Recall**: Close the solution and try to implement from memory
3. **Spaced Repetition**: Revisit problems after 1 day, 1 week, 1 month
4. **Teach Others**: Explain solutions to solidify understanding
5. **Track Mistakes**: Maintain an error journal
