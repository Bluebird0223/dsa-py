## Tips for Maximum Learning

1. **Try before looking**: Spend at least 30 minutes trying to solve on your own
2. **Multiple solutions**: After solving, find alternative approaches
3. **Space optimization**: Always try to optimize space complexity
4. **Explain it**: Write out the logic in comments
5. **Revisit**: Come back to hard problems after 1 week
